How to Read This

MAE and RMSE measure forecast error in units sold. MAPE shows relative error as a percentage. Lower values mean the forecast matched the hold-out data more closely.

Why this helps your final defense

This page gives you objective model evaluation, not just predictions. You can show that the forecast is tested against hold-out data, compare MAE/RMSE/MAPE, and explain how error changes by product.

  • • Shows model quality with measurable metrics.
  • • Demonstrates reproducible evaluation from saved forecast files.
  • • Makes it easier to justify why the forecasting module is reliable enough for deployment.
